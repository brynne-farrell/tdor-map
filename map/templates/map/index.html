About this Map

This map is best viewed full screen on a desktop display

This map shows the deaths of transgender folks over the year 2017 (from 2016/11/20 to 2017/11/20). Each week of the year passes in about one second. You can control the playback with the buttons at the top of the page.

When each death happens, it starts as a solid red dot, which slowly fades over the course of a month.

Date and location of deaths sourced from Abernathey, M. Memorializing 2017. Retrieved Nov 20, 2017 from https://tdor.info/.

Date and location of deaths were automatically parsed and located using Google's Geocoding API and have not been reviewed for accuracy
