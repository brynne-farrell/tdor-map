  • 2018 (current)

This map shows the deaths of transgender folks over the year 2018 (from 2017/11/20 to 2018/11/20). Each week of the year passes in about one second. You can control the playback with the buttons at the top of the page.

As the year is played, each death starts as a solid red dot, which slowly fades over the course of a month.

Data sourced from Abernathey, M. Memorializing 2018. Retrieved Dec 4, 2018 from https://tdor.info/.

Name, date, and location of deaths were automatically parsed and located using a custom script and Google's Geocoding API and have not been reviewed for accuracy.

This map is best viewed full screen on a desktop display.
